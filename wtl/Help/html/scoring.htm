How does the scoring work?

The standard score for a piece is 6 at level one.

This standard score is increased by 6 with every subsequent level.

A point is deducted for every keystroke pressed.

One point is awarded for every free space that the piece is dropped.

Points are deducted for displaying the "piece preview" 2 points are initially deducted and an additional 2 for every subsequent level

A minimum of 4 points will be awarded.

100 points are awarded for sinking 1 rows
200 points are awarded for sinking 2 rows
400 points are awarded for sinking 3 rows
800 points are awarded for sinking 4 rows
1600 points are awarded for sinking 5 rows
