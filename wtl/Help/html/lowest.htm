Welcome to StackPack

Stackup is a clone of that famous Tetris game

Why another Tetris clone?

StackPack was written as a programming exercise. The rules are clearly defined, so I could focus purely on development.

There were "essential" things that the version I was playing at the time could not do.

Aims of this version

  • Practical and playable
  • Customisable
  • Non intrusive
  • Small and fast

History

StackPack started out as an MFC program, it was later ported to a WTL program.
